The Couple

Michelle Wang

Everyone who knows Michelle will tell you that she's one of the sweetest people you'll ever meet. Of course, get to know her a little while longer, and you'll see that she's sassy and loves to prank you. The part of her that doesn't change is her love for God and wanting to be obedient to Him.

Michael Wang

A quick impression of Michael will lead you to think that he's energetic, passionate, extremely positive and a huge dork. While he may thinks that he's none of these things, especially the last one, he's quick to point the glory to God for any of this.

Q & A

  • What do you like the most about the other person?
  • Michael: Her sincerity, sensibility, and humor. She's cracks me up all the time. She also has really childish expressions that I find very endearing.
  • Michelle: Michael is 99% of the time a very cheerful and upbeat guy. He rarely complains. He sees the best in every situation and in every person. He's also a very patient listener who takes the time and effort to understand what a person is going through.
  • What is your favorite memory together?
  • Michael: It's not a particular event, but I feel the most loved by Michelle when she asks me to do something again. Whether that be take another photo, saying that I love her, or writing her a card.
  • Michelle: Gah, so many! I'll share two of them: one of my favorite memories of Michael is how he found ways to physically care for me despite our #strongdistance relationship. For example, when I got sick (quite frequently), he would PostMate me a meal or ask a brother to get me wholesome orange juice. Secondly, that two hour times difference between us? He used it to his advantage and would give me a wake up call every workday morning to ensure that I wouldn't oversleep (not that I ever did or anything...).
  • How do you like to spend time together?
  • Michael: I love it when both of us use our gifts to serve God and the church!
  • Michelle: Anything where we can have fun interacting with one another over new experiences, such as the trying fun restaurants, touring Google headquarters, or trying rock climbing for the first time.
  • What makes this other person unique?
  • Michael: her tolerance of my many short-comings and insecurities. Her ability to love me, not because I'm inherently lovable (trust me, the more you know about me the more you'll believe that!), but because God has loved her first and forgave her and has worked in her heart to love a sinner like me.
  • Michelle: He usually has his own personal, special nickname for each of his friends. Or he has a certain way he says certain people's names. If you aren't familiar with it, just hang around him long enough and you'll find out soon enough!
  • What's one thing you don't understand about him/her?
  • Michael: I don't understand why she likes shopping so much... Jokes aside, I don't understand how she has such an intuitive understanding of peoples' feelings and desires.
  • Michelle: Michael says I laugh a lot at him, and that's because sometimes, I just don't understand the complexity of how his mind works. For example, he would incorporate Chinese phrases into conversations that he translated from English slang. For example, "Jokes, dude," becomes "笑話, dude".
  • Why do you want to marry each other?
  • Michael: because she's someone who will be able to help me paint a wonderful picture of Christ and church. Her gifts complement mine very well and I've benefitted so much from her wisdom, support, and rebukes.
  • Michelle: I want to marry Michael because he loves the Lord above all else. It's evident in the way that he prays, the way that he earnestly shares the gospel, the way he treats others with love, and the way he prioritizes the church. Because he walks closely with our Savior, Jesus Christ, Michael is able to die to himself and his own desires, instead choosing to serves others needs above his own. Michael is far from perfect, but he is eager to learn and willing to change through wise counsel. I've seen him grow into different roles of responsibility that God has given him, and I'm so thankful he is willing to take leadership of future little family. I cannot wait to marry you, Michael!

Our Story

"'Michelle Wang'? You're like the girl version of me!" - Michael's first words to Michelle

  • First We Meet

    December 2012

    Michael and Michelle first met at a joint college fellowship event. He thought she was cute. She thought he looked like KevJumba. Feelings quickly grew on his end as he observed her hospitality toward their classmates and desire to learn more about God.

  • First Try

    October 2013

    In their sophomore year, Michael decided to ask Michelle out privately at the end of their sophomore year all-church retreat. She wasn't particularly attracted to him and turned him down the next day. Right before their Korean Pop Music Midterm. He never stopped liking her.

  • Feelings Develop

    October 2013 - February 2015

    In the meantime, Michael and Michelle were both growing in the Lord in their separate ways. However, it was God's plan that they kept crossing paths, almost every day in fact. Michael and Michelle found themselves in almost all of the same church ministries, school clubs and social circles. Feelings began developing on Michelle's end, and Michael was beginning to think about dating again...

  • First Date

    March 2015

    Michael decided to ask Michelle out on a date. This was super surprising to her because she remembers him telling her after his internship in New York that he wasn't interested in dating! She was a lot more receptive this time. The two met up with some older counselors, and began officially dating.

  • Long Distance

    June 2015

    After three months of dating in San Diego, Michael graduated and got a job in Chicago. Both him and Michelle were sad about the prospect of continuing their relationship apart, but Michelle decided to stay with Michael and so the two began their long-distance relationship.

  • Proposal

    April 2017

    After two long years of cross-country flights, fun dates, relaxing dates and hard dates, Michael decided to put a ring on it. He (sort of) surprised her at Mount Soledad, the location of their first date, and asked her to marry her. She said okay.

  • Presently

    April 2017 - January 2018

    Michael and Michelle are having lots of fun planning for their wedding in San Diego and preparing for their future lives together in Chicago! They can't wait to see you there.
